Compared to free solutions like Google's Colaboratory, SIPB JupyterHub provides much longer kernel timeouts, faster kernel startup times, access to your MIT AFS files in your ~/Jupyter folder, and a much wider variety of kernels.

Currently, we support the following kernels:

  • Python 3.8.5
  • Node.js v10
  • Typescript 3.9
  • Julia 1.4.1
  • R 3.6
  • GNU Octave
  • PARI/GP


If you have any questions, comments, requests for new kernels/packages, or want to help us out, drop us an email at sipb-cloud (at) mit.edu!
